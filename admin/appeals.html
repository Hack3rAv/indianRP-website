APPEALS SYSTEM

Review appeals submitted by players regarding punishments or administrative actions.

APPEAL REVIEW

Appeals are reviewed by authorized appeal administrators. Each appeal is evaluated based on evidence, logs, and rules.

  • • Appeal ID format: APL-XXXXXX
  • • Linked to player ID and punishment record
  • • Status: Open / Under Review / Accepted / Rejected
  • • Final decision recorded in audit logs
