Audit Logs

SENIOR ADMIN

Administrative Audit Trail

This section records all critical administrative actions, including punishments, appeal decisions, role changes, and system-level interventions.

  • Timestamped entries
  • Admin ID and rank
  • Action type and target
  • Reason and evidence reference

Audit logs are permanent and cannot be edited or deleted. Any misuse of administrative authority is fully traceable.

Audit data will be populated automatically once backend logging is enabled.

Accountability Notice

Abuse of power or attempts to bypass logging systems will result in immediate disciplinary action.
