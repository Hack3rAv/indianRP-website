Official documentation for Indian Roleplay. Rules, systems, and guides every player must know.

1. GENERAL ROLEPLAY RULES

1.1 Stay In Character

CORE

Players must remain in character at all times. Out-of-character communication in voice or local chat is strictly prohibited.

1.2 Powergaming

PUNISHABLE

Performing actions that are unrealistic or force roleplay on others without giving them a chance to respond.

2. COMBAT RULES

2.1 Deathmatching (DM)

STRICT

Killing another player without a valid roleplay reason is forbidden and will result in punishment.

2.2 New Life Rule (NLR)

IMPORTANT

After death, a player must forget all events leading to that situation and may not return for a defined time.
